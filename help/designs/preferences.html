Setting Preferences

1. Setting Business Preferences

Business Preferences in RSF are used to specify preferences pertaining to pricing of designs. To edit business preferences, go to Preferences | Business Preferences. After editing, the changes can be saved.

It is possible to revert to previous settings by selecting the date in the Business Preference History box and saving it again. All the business settings will then roll back to the settings of the selected date and will be used in the designs that follow.

See the definitions section for the definitions of the business preference items.


2. Setting Method Preferences

It is possible to revert to previous settings by selecting the date in the Method Preferences History box and saving it again. All the method settings will then roll back to the settings of the selected date and will be used in the designs that follow.

See the definitions section for the definitions of the method preference items.
