Opening an existing design

Open existing designs by selecting File | Open Designs or click on the open design button on the main tool bar. The open designs dialog will be displayed. Search for the designs you want to open and select them by checking the check boxes in the left column. Click on select when finished.

To aid in your search to find the designs you want to open, you can sort the existing designs by code, title or date. You can also filter the designs according to status.
