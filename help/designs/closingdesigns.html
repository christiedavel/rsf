Closing current designs

Close designs that are currently open by selecting File | Close designs or click on the close designs button on the main tool bar. The close designs dialog will be displayed. Search for the designs you want to close and select them by checking the check boxes in the left column. Click on Select when finished.

To aid in your search to find the designs you want to close, you can sort the existing designs by code, title or date. You can also filter the designs according to status.
