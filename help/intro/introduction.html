Introduction

Welcome to RSF. It is a well known fact that picture framing is a labour intensive process. Add to this the fact that the picture framer spends a great deal of time consulting with clients and needs to be able to make swift stock decisions in order to give professional advice on availability and price, and one begins to understand why it is difficult to have adequate control over the amounts of materials used.

We at RSF understand the problems that picture framers face and we are confident that RSF will bring a new dimension to your business in that it will provide a means to speed up and simplify the design and quotation process. In addition it will also give the business owner improved control over stock.
