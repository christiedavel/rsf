Registration by Phone

This option is provided for users that do not have internet access. In order to register, proceed with the following steps:

  1. Select Settings | Registration | By phone.

  2. Call the registration telephone number provided on the CD cover.

  3. The operator will ask some questions about your business to verify that the information we have is correct.

  4. Enter the base codes 1 and 2 which will be provided by the operator.

  5. Read the ID Number and registration codes 1 and 2 to the operator.

  6. Enter the result code which will be provided by the operator.

  7. Click on the Register button to complete the process.
